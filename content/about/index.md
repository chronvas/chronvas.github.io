## Short bio
I am an Android/Mobile Software Engineer with extensive experience in building high-quality mobile applications. Currently, I am a Senior Software Engineer at the BBC, where I have contributed to major apps like BBC Weather and BBC News, driving technical decisions in areas like A/B testing infrastructure and migrating from XML to Jetpack Compose.

I have a proven track record of leading engineering teams and architecting core application layers. My expertise includes Kotlin, Java, and C#, with a strong focus on modernizing legacy systems and implementing scalable architectures using MVVM, RxJava, and Jetpack Compose. Prior co-founder of Figata, a health-tech startup that won 1st prize at the First Startup Weekend Larissa.
